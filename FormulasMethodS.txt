how to find the angle of a triangle given 3 sides
--------------------------------------------------
result1 = ((b*b) + (c*c) - (a*a)) / (2 * (b*c));
result2 = acos(result1);
result2 *= (180/3.1416);
/* acos() = . . . radian, convert it to degree,
   degree = radian * (180 / pi) */
